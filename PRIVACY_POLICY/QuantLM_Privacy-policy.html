QuantLM Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Thank you for using QuantLM. Your privacy is important. QuantLM is designed with a privacy-first approach and performs nearly all processing locally on your device. The developer does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information.

Information We Collect

QuantLM does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal information.

  • No account is required.
  • No login is required.
  • No analytics or user tracking is performed.
  • No personal information is stored on developer-owned servers.

Internet Usage

Internet access is used only for features that require online connectivity, including:

  • Downloading AI language models.
  • Downloading application updates or supporting resources.
  • Performing web searches using supported search providers (such as DuckDuckGo).
  • Accessing online AI providers or APIs that you explicitly choose to use.

When these features are used, your device communicates directly with the relevant third-party service. QuantLM does not collect, inspect, or store the contents of these communications.

Permissions

Camera

Used only for image capture and AI vision features when you choose to use them.

Microphone

Used only for voice input, speech recognition, and audio-related AI features.

Photos & Videos

Used only to allow you to select, import, analyze, or process media files that you choose.

Storage

Used only to import, export, save, or manage AI models, conversations, documents, and other user-selected files.

Calendar

Used only for calendar-related AI features, such as viewing, creating, editing, or managing events with your permission. Calendar information remains on your device unless you explicitly choose to use a third-party calendar service.

Internet

Required solely for online functionality such as downloading AI models, performing web searches, accessing online AI services that you configure, retrieving online resources, and checking for updates.

Permission Usage

All permissions are optional. If you deny a permission, only the associated feature may be unavailable. The remainder of the application will continue to function whenever possible.

Local Data Storage

QuantLM stores its working data locally on your device. This may include:

  • Downloaded AI models
  • Conversation history
  • Application settings
  • User preferences
  • User-selected files

This information remains on your device. The developer does not upload or access this data.

Third-Party Services

QuantLM does not include:

  • Firebase
  • Google Analytics
  • Advertising SDKs
  • User tracking SDKs
  • Developer-operated cloud storage

Optional features may communicate directly with external services such as AI providers, model repositories, or search providers. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

Advertising

QuantLM does not display advertisements.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share user data.

Data Security

QuantLM is designed to process data locally whenever possible, helping keep your information on your own device.

Children's Privacy

QuantLM is not specifically intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page together with the updated revision date.
